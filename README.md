### The best way to reach me is to open an issue. Pick the form that fits and it walks you through everything that's needed. An answer may take some time, apologies for that. I appreciate every submission!

**→ [Open a new issue](https://github.com/Serilum/.issue-tracker/issues/new/choose)**

<br><br>

![Checklist](https://cdn.serilum.com/description/nameplate/checklist.svg)
### Please keep a few things in mind:

- **Tell me the mod name.**
  - This issue tracker is used for all of Serilum's mods. To avoid confusion, please specify the name.
- **Have a quick look at the [FAQ](https://github.com/Serilum/.information/wiki/FAQ) first.**
  - Common questions about supported versions, timeframes, downloading and mod configuration may already be answered there.
- **Make sure you are on the latest mod version and a supported Minecraft version.**
  - The issue you ran into may already be fixed.
